Team

Charles Oppenheimer

Founder and CEO

Charles Oppenheimer is the founder of Oppenheimer Energy and a leading voice for a cooperative framework of science and technology. Grandson of J. Robert Oppenheimer, Charles has dedicated his life to ensuring that nuclear science serves humanity, moving us away from nuclear weapons. He is a digital design leader, systems thinker, and strategic convener who brings together government, industry, and civil society to reimagine nuclear energy as a cornerstone of global development.

Connect with Charles.

Ryan Pickering

Executive Director

Ryan Pickering is a nuclear energy developer and policy strategist with experience across utility-scale power plants, grassroots advocacy, and federal energy policy. He leads Oppenheimer Energy’s development and public engagement. A speaker at COP28 and advisor to nuclear utilities and regulators, Ryan is known for translating complexity into momentum. He previously led successful campaigns to extend the life of California’s Diablo Canyon nuclear plant and co-founded the Nuclear is Clean Energy Club at UC Berkeley.

Connect with Ryan.

Duane Olcsvary

Head of Nuclear

Duane Olcsvary is a veteran nuclear executive with decades of experience leading nuclear project planning, nuclear program management, and commercial project management for global nuclear projects. As Head of Nuclear at Oppenheimer Energy, Duane oversees technical diligence, utility interface, and compliance strategy. He brings a deep understanding of NRC processes, nuclear construction risk, and the unique demands of regulated and deregulated markets. Duane is a trusted voice in utility boardrooms and a mentor to the next generation of nuclear professionals.
